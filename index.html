VEGAPI

An accounting and tax API for business application developers

VEGAPI is an hosted API for developers of business applications, that handles accounting and taxes for every document, payment or bank transaction, that you post to it.

Using VEGAPI you can enhance your business applications by providing accounting and tax functionalities to your customers.

Integration with VEGAPI is simple: your application talks to VEGAPI via HTTPS transactions, sending or receiving JSON objects and using familiar HTTP headers and return codes.

The initial version of the API implements the following resources:

  • /companies : any entity using your application to manage its business - basically a Company is your customer
  • /documents : any document - invoice, order, delivery note, expense report... - that a Company issues or receives
  • /payments : any payment made or received by a Company
  • /cash-transactions : any financial transaction made by a Company on its money accounts (banks and cashier)
  • /entities : any 3rd party with whom a Company has business - customers, suppliers, employees
  • /items : any product or service sold or purchased by a Company
  • /accounting : a set of resources that support the accounting process of a Company, allowing your application to generate manual accounting entries, review accounting errors, prepare acounting reports and handle other actions like closing a fiscal year
  • /vat : a set of resources allowing your application to generate and review VAT reports for a Company
  • /settings : a set of resources that allow your application to drive the accounting processes of a Company.

You can look at the documentation, experiment the sample API or look at the source code, if you feel like it.

You can use the hosted version of VEGAPI once it is available (planned for Apr'2015) under a simple pricing plan based on the amount of documents posted. Upon subscribing to the hosted version you will receive an identifier and authentication key for your application.


VEGAPI is being designed and developed by a team of people with several years of experience in the field of business application design and development in several industries - manufacturing, distribution, retail, financial services and telecoms / ISP.
